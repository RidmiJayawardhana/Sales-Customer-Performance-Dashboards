# 📊 Sales & Customer Performance Dashboards (Tableau)

## 📌 Overview

This project presents interactive **Sales and Customer Dashboards** built using Tableau to analyze business performance and customer behavior.
The dashboards provide actionable insights through KPI tracking, trend analysis, and dynamic filtering, enabling data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze **year-over-year (YoY) sales performance**
* Identify **sales trends and patterns**
* Understand **customer behavior and segmentation**
* Highlight **top-performing products and customers**

---

## 📈 Sales Dashboard

### 🔹 Features

* **KPI Overview**

  * Total Sales
  * Total Profit
  * Total Quantity (Current vs Previous Year)

* **Sales Trends**

  * Monthly comparison of KPIs
  * Identification of highest & lowest performing months

* **Product Subcategory Analysis**

  * Sales comparison across subcategories
  * Profit vs Sales insights

* **Weekly Trends**

  * Weekly Sales & Profit visualization
  * Average benchmarks
  * Highlighting above/below average performance

---

## 👥 Customer Dashboard

### 🔹 Features

* **KPI Overview**

  * Total Customers
  * Sales per Customer
  * Total Orders (Current vs Previous Year)

* **Customer Trends**

  * Monthly performance tracking
  * Peak and low activity periods

* **Customer Distribution**

  * Analysis based on number of orders
  * Insights into loyalty and engagement

* **Top 10 Customers**

  * Ranked by profit contribution
  * Includes:

    * Sales
    * Profit
    * Number of Orders
    * Last Order Date

---

## ⚙️ Interactivity & Filters

* Dynamic **year selection** for historical analysis
* Interactive charts with **drill-down capabilities**
* Filters available for:

  * Product Category & Subcategory
  * Region, State, City

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* **Tableau** – Data visualization & dashboard design
* Data Cleaning & Preparation (within Tableau / source dataset)
